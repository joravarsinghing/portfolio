About me

A lifelong tinkerer with an insatiable curiosity about how the world works. From figuring out the magic behind the TV remote to unraveling the workings of a jet engine, I've always been driven to understand the inner workings of things.

My fascination with intricate machinery, especially fast automobiles and aircrafts, led me to combine my technical skills with artistic flair. That's how I landed on becoming an engineer – a creator who shapes the future, not just analyzes it.

Through my experiences in both mechanical and mechatronics engineering, I've developed a well-rounded skillset, including:

  • • Designing hardware, 3D modeling, and generative design
  • • Building logic into systems, whether it's code, circuits, or components
  • • Simulating real-world scenarios like stress tests, aerodynamics, and user experiences
  • • Documenting and reporting on project objectives, observations, and results
